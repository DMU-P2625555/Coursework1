FURY

SYNOPSIS

FURY, tells the gritty story of five men from the US Army’s Armoured Division, attempt to put a stop to the Nazi regime as they roll through western German countryside.

In the final days of the Second World War the Allies push the German War Machine ever closer towards Berlin, yet the victors of this war still hang in the balance with American soldiers on their last fighting legs – as the end draws nearer, five unlikely companions from the Second Armoured Division push their minds, bodies and their Tank ‘Fury’ to their absolute limits.

‘Wardaddy’ (Brad Pitt) is joined by the likes of ‘Bible’ Swan (Shia LaBeouf), Grady Travis (Jon Bernthal) and ‘Gordo’ Garcia (Michael Peña) and ‘Red.’

For them, the war began in Northern Africa, from there: Italy, and then to France, but just as their caterpillar tracks cross the French-German border, Red is killed. It is now up to newbie: Norman Ellison (Logan Lerman), a V-Corps Clerk Typist, thrown into the hands of these battle hardened men, to steer Fury and her insatiable crew to victory.
